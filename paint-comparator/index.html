Paint comparator


Have you ever seen a miniature online and wished you knew how to pick the colours? Pick the colour you want in hex form and plug it into the search below to get the closest color paint matched. Pick from Citadel and Vellejo (more to come!)
NOTE: You can sort by clicking on the table's headings. Its not a perfect system, but it's something!
FUN FACT: This uses a mathematics formula called 'Euclidean Distance' to determine the closeness between colors.
You can use an Online Color Picker (like this one here) if you're unsure how to pick colours.

Search with Hex, RGB or HSL

(e.g. #7e1b55, rgb(128,40,40), hsl(342,24%,38%))
